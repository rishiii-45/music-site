About DopeTracks

DopeTracks is a simple web music project made with love for music lovers. Built by a web designing student (that’s me 👋), this site features some of the best Indian music artists and curated playlists to vibe to.

It’s not a professional product, but a fun and passionate experiment to learn and create something cool. Enjoy the tunes, and thanks for stopping by!
